### Foundation Reserves

Until Foundation Reserves are exhausted, 150,000 new tokens will be minted and added to the amount of Truss Tokens distributed to Foundation account in any 24-hour period.

### New User Bounties

New user bounties are paid to the entities that create or consume new Truss Security Products. This bounty is paid automatically and in addition to normal fee distribution and is available to any entity that creates or consumes a Truss Security Product.

The new user bounties are currently set to 40% of the purchase price paid by the consumer entity and will be paid until New User Bounty pool is exhausted.
